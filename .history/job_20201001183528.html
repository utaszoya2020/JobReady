Online Customer Service - Retail

Kitchen Warehouse Pty Ltd

More jobs from this company

Kitchen Warehouse is Australia's largest kitchenware retailer. We are looking for highly motivated and enthusiastic staff who have a passion for genuine customer service.

Location

Our support office and WA warehouse are located in Bibra Lake with easy access off the Kwinana Freeway.

The Position

We interact with our customers by phone, email and live chat. This position is the key to establishing and maintaining happy, satisfied customers who are confident and comfortable with our level of service and care. This role suits an applicant who is enthusiastic, organised, loves interacting with people and has a genuine desire to help our customers.

Tasks

Interacting with customers by phone, email and live chat in a friendly, supportive and efficient way.
Processing orders
Processing customer returns
Communicating with suppliers re product queries
Communicating with our stores re customer orders/enquiries
Reviewing orders and advising customers of progress or action taken.

Requirements

A strong customer service background is a must for this position. We're hoping to fill the role asap, but are more interested in making sure it's a good fit and we end up with the right person, so, if you match the criteria, here's what you need to do: Apply, including a cover letter outlining why you feel this position would suit your skill base and how you will add value to our customer service team. You may want to visit us at www.kitchenwarehouse.com.au
